What We Do

Our Services

From routine maintenance to full custom builds — we handle every aspect of your 4x4 and drivetrain needs.

Differential Repair & Rebuild

Complete differential overhaul and repair services for all makes and models. Precision work you can trust.

Ring & Pinion Installation

Expert gear setup and ring & pinion installation. Proper backlash and pattern every time.

Locker Installs

Professional installation of selectable and automatic lockers for maximum off-road traction.

Axle Service

Full axle service including axle shaft replacement, bearing and seal jobs, and axle rebuilds.

Gear Setup

Precision gear setup and adjustment. Proper contact patterns, backlash specs, and preload settings.

4x4 Upgrades

Custom 4x4 upgrades, lift kits, and drivetrain modifications to take your rig to the next level.

Transfer Cases

Transfer case repair, rebuild, and upgrades for all 4x4 platforms. Chain, gear, and bearing service.

Transmissions

Manual and automatic transmission service. We handle both standard and performance builds.

Clutch Services

Complete clutch replacement and repair services for manual transmission vehicles.

About Lara & Sons

A family-owned business built on decades of hands-on expertise in differentials and 4x4 service.

Family-Owned & Proud

Satisfaction GUARANTEED

Lara & Sons Differentials & 4x4 has been serving the Jurupa Valley community and beyond with professional drivetrain and 4x4 service. We specialize in differential repair, rebuild, and custom 4x4 work that keeps trucks, Jeeps, and off-road rigs performing at their best.

From gear setup and ring & pinion installation to locker installs and axle service — we bring decades of hands-on expertise to every job. Whether you drive a daily commuter or a full rock-crawling build, we treat every vehicle like it's our own.
