Humble Beginnings

In 1980, Mr. Fermin Lara started working for Lucas Transmission as an extra helping hand. With a hunger to learn what was going on around him, he quickly went from helper to lead differential builder.

Unfortunately Lucas was going through hard times in the early 1990's. Cuts had to be made, again, and again. Eventually the shop was broken down into 3 departments.

Mr. Lara's department — the differential department — performed so well that he was able to purchase the rights to the entire shop.

In 1994, Lara & Sons Differentials was born. Six years later, the family business became incorporated.

Today, with over 30 years in business and working with the likes of AT&T, PRN Ambulance, and the L.A. County Sheriff's Department, we'd like to add your name to our list of satisfied customers.
