Lara & Sons Differentials & 4x4 has been serving the Jurupa Valley community and beyond with professional drivetrain and 4x4 service. We specialize in differential repair, rebuild, and custom 4x4 work that keeps trucks, Jeeps, and off-road rigs performing at their best.

From gear setup and ring & pinion installation to locker installs and axle service — we bring decades of hands-on expertise to every job. Whether you drive a daily commuter or a full rock-crawling build, we treat every vehicle like it's our own.
